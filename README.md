**Summary**:

After his marriage collapses, Raiden Jack finds himself trapped in a mind that cannot forget. Every memory remains archived, every mistake replaying without mercy.

One quiet morning by a lake, he almost finds a way to silence it all.

Instead, in the broken reflection of the water, he finds someone who pulls him back.

What begins as a strange companionship slowly becomes something far more complicated. Because sometimes healing feels like one step forward and three steps back.

Twelve months later, he would learn that some relationships do not end quietly, and that sometimes the only way to stop moving in circles is to walk away in a way no one expects.
